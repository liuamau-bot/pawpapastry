Our Story

Your pets are
family too.

Our fur babies are family — and we only want the best for them. We have a dog of our own, but she's never been much of a treat lover. That made us think: if we're going to give her something, it had better be genuinely wholesome — no additives, nothing unnecessary, nothing we wouldn't feel good about.

So we started researching, taking classes, and developing recipes. After a lot of testing (and a lot of taste-testing from our very discerning dog), PawPa Pastry was born.

The name PawPa comes from our dog, Pasta. We use only all-natural, additive-free ingredients, and every treat and cake is made fresh to order — because freshness is something you can't fake. Let's live happily with our dogs. 🐾
